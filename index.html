What I Believe

My core principles that guide every article and advocacy effort

Teenage Girls Deserve Agency Over Their Bodies. Period.

Comprehensive sexuality education and access to reproductive health services are fundamental rights, not privileges to be earned.

Education in Emergencies is Non-Negotiable

Conflict and displacement amplify vulnerabilities - safe learning spaces must be prioritized in humanitarian response.

Policy Without Implementation is Empty Rhetoric

African governments must move beyond ratifying conventions to funding and enforcing protections for girls.

Data Must Drive Decisions

Meaningful change requires disaggregated data that reveals the unique challenges facing girls in different contexts.
